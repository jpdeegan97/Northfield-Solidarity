# 002 TAXONOMY

## Entities
- Business
- Lead
- Contact (role inbox / publicly listed person)
- Intent Signal (permit/RFP/hiring/press/etc.)
- Evidence (URL + capture + extracts)
- CWP Objects (Company Report, Personnel Map, Conversion Timeline, Outreach Assets)

## Sensitivity tiers
- Tier 0: public business info
- Tier 1: public person info (names/titles) with evidence
- Tier 2: direct contact info (lawful public/consented)
- Tier 3: regulated financial/personal (credit score, bankruptcy details) — restricted; disabled by default

## CWP concepts
- **Company Report (CR):** evidence-backed context + triggers
- **Personnel Map (PM):** stakeholder roles + contact paths (public/professional only)
- **Conversion Timeline (CT):** sequenced touches with conditions/fallbacks
- **Value Artifact:** helpful deliverable (mini-audit, benchmark, teardown)

## Stop/slow conditions
- opt-out / do-not-contact
- negative response (“no”)
- low confidence / ambiguous entity
- compliance risk flag
