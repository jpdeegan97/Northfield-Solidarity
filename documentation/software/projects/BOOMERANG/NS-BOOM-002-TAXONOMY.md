## Core Concepts
- **RunSpec:** the canonical specification for a probe.
- **Market Slice:** narrow ICP + channel + geo + context.
- **Strategy Template:** reusable approach playbook.
- **Offer:** the thing being validated (service, waitlist, paid pilot).
- **Asset Bundle:** landing pages, copy, scripts, creatives, demo.
- **Timebox:** fixed execution window (e.g., 72h, 7d, 14d).
- **Telemetry:** standardized measurement schema for outcomes.
- **Signal:** evidence that the market responds (lead, click, call, $).
- **Decision Gate:** rule that triggers next actions.
- **Iteration Loop:** bounded adjustments during a run.

## Strategy Classes (starter)
- **Outbound:** cold email/DM, targeted lists, follow-ups
- **Inbound Content:** SEO wedge, social threads, community posts
- **Paid Search/Social:** micro-budget ads, keyword probes
- **Partnership Wedge:** integrations, affiliate, channel partners
- **Community Seeding:** building-level, city-level, niche community
- **Product-Led Probe:** fake-door, waitlist, interactive demo
- **Service-first:** concierge MVP to validate demand and pricing

## Outcome Classes
- **Interest:** clicks, saves, signups, replies
- **Intent:** booking requests, qualified leads, demos scheduled
- **Revenue:** paid pilots, deposits, subscriptions
- **Friction:** objections, churn, refund, negative sentiment
- **Channel health:** CPM/CPC volatility, deliverability, moderation risk
