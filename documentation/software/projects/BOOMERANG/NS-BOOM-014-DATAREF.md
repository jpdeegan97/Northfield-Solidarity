# NS-BOOM-014-DATAREF — Data References & Interfaces

## Inputs
- Market research (DRE units)
- Strategy templates (library)
- Channel configs (keywords/audiences/lists)
- Assets (landing pages, copy, creatives)
- Telemetry feeds (analytics, ad spend, CRM pipeline, comms logs)

## Outputs
- Structured run reports (markdown/json)
- Scorecards (normalized)
- Artifact bundles (versioned)
- Outcome records for Market × Strategy learning

## Standard KPIs (starter)
- **Funnel:** views → clicks → leads → qualified → close
- **Economics:** spend, CPC/CPM, CAC proxy, time-to-first-lead, time-to-first-$
- **Quality:** lead quality score, objection density, sentiment
- **Channel health:** deliverability, moderation flags, volatility

## Governance Notes
- Outreach must respect platform TOS and anti-spam rules.
- Data handling policy defines what PII is stored and how.
- Clear opt-out and suppression lists for outbound.
