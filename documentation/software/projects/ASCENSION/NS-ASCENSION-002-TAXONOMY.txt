NS-ASCENSION-002-TAXONOMY

Core Entities
Role: an open position with competencies and gate-specific evaluation criteria.
Candidate: a person progressing through gates.
Gate: a stage with entry/exit rules and rubrics.
Artifact: proof produced by a candidate (portfolio, writing, code, demo, etc.).
Scorecard: structured evaluation at a gate.
Decision: pass/hold/close with reasons.
Offer: final package details (comp, start, contingencies).
Gate Map
Gate 0 — The Signal: Application as a ‘ping’ from outside the Firmament.
Gate 1 — The Echo: Async proof: portfolio, written answers, or short loom.
Gate 2 — The Trial: A small, paid challenge (time-boxed, scoped, repeatable).
Gate 3 — The Alignment: Operating style + values + communication calibration.
Gate 4 — The Door: Final live session: deep dive + mutual fit.
Gate 5 — The Center: The final landing: intentional conversation + decision.
Competency categories (suggested)
Craft (quality and correctness)
Judgment (tradeoffs, prioritization, constraints)
Velocity (time-to-signal and pragmatic execution)
Communication (clarity, empathy, precision)
Taste (product/UX sensibility where applicable)
Ownership (initiative and reliability)
