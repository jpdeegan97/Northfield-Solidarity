NS-ASCENSION-009-RUNBOOK

Runbook: operating Ascension
1) Intake
Review Gate 0 submissions daily.
Respond with either Gate 1 prompt or a clear close.
2) Echo evaluation
Score via rubric; store decision reasons.
If pass: issue Trial package and payment terms.
3) Trial
Send trial brief + deadline + payment terms.
On submission: acknowledge receipt; schedule review window.
Complete scorecard; decide pass/close.
4) Alignment + Door + Center
Run alignment call with set questions.
Deep dive on trial; ensure mutual expectations.
Final Center conversation: decision + next steps.
Quality bar controls
Calibrate rubrics monthly (reduce evaluator drift).
Keep trials consistent; vary only when role demands it.
Keep a golden role template and scorecard baseline.
