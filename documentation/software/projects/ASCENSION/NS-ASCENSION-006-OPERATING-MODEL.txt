NS-ASCENSION-006-OPERATING-MODEL

Core roles
Owner (Center): final decision maker; maintains mythos and bar.
Gatekeeper: runs the pipeline, comms, and scheduling.
Evaluators: role-specific reviewers; fill scorecards.
Ops: payments for trials, doc handling, and records.
Cadence (rhythm)
Daily: review new signals and move candidates forward or close quickly.
Weekly: pipeline review, role updates, publish changes via CONCORD.
Monthly: calibrate rubrics and adjust trial scopes/rates.
SLA targets
Gate 0 response: 72 hours
Gate 1 evaluation: 5 days
Gate 2 trial review: 5 days
Offer decision after Gate 4: 72 hours
