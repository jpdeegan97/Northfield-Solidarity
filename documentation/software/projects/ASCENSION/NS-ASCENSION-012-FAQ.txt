FAQ (draft)
How long does Ascension take? Typical: 1–3 weeks depending on gate timing and role.
Is the Trial paid? Yes for meaningful work; details per role.
Do you give feedback? Concise feedback always; deeper feedback after paid work when possible.
Can I skip gates? Rare; only if prior signal is overwhelming and role demands it.
What if I’m busy? We can pause; the gates are designed to be async-friendly.
