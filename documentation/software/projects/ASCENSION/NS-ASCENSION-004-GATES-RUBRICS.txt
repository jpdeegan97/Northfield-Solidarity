NS-ASCENSION-004-GATES-RUBRICS

Gate 0 — The Signal
Input: resume/LinkedIn, portfolio, short intro, role preference.
Goal: filter for baseline fit and genuine interest.
Pass criteria: clear work evidence + alignment to role scope.
Rubric (0–4 each)
Relevance to role
Evidence of shipping
Communication clarity
Motivation fit
Gate 1 — The Echo (Async proof)
Input: 4–6 questions (written) or a 3–5 minute loom.
Goal: confirm thinking style and clarity.
Timebox: 30–45 minutes.
Rubric (0–4 each)
Structured thinking
Tradeoff awareness
Conciseness
Truthfulness (flags uncertainty honestly)
Gate 2 — The Trial (Paid)
Input: a scoped, paid project aligned to the role.
Goal: observe real execution under constraints.
Timebox: 2–6 hours depending on role; paid at a published rate.
Deliverable: artifact + short README + optional demo.
Rubric (0–4 each)
Correctness / quality
Pragmatism
Documentation / handoff
Taste / user empathy (where applicable)
Speed-to-signal
Gate 3 — The Alignment
Format: 30–45 minute conversation.
Goal: verify operating style, collaboration, and alignment with how NS works.
Topics: autonomy, feedback loops, disagreement style, reliability.
Rubric (0–4 each)
Ownership mindset
Feedback receptivity
Communication under ambiguity
Values alignment
Gate 4 — The Door (Deep dive)
Format: 60–90 minutes (live).
Goal: go deep into the trial or a prior project; stress-test judgment.
Output: final scorecard + offer recommendation.
Gate 5 — The Center (Final landing)
Format: in-person if possible; otherwise a deliberate final session.
Goal: final mutual decision and expectation setting.
Output: offer or clear close.
