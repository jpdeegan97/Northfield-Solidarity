Prop Entity Creation Automation (PECA)

1. Engine Identity

Engine Name: Prop Entity Creation Automation

Acronym: PECA

Engine Type: Entity Formation / Automation

2. Mission Statement

Automate the creation of property-holding entities and link them to NSDC governance and identity systems.

3. Core Responsibilities

  • Automatically create property entities (LLCs, sub-entities)
  • Assign canonical IDs via IDN
  • Log entity creation and notify GGP for compliance
  • Maintain entity lifecycle audit trails

4. Non-Responsibilities

  • Track financial performance (PTE, FLO)
  • Make investment or acquisition decisions
  • Publish content or perform market research

5. Inputs

6. Outputs

7. Authority & Decision Rights

  • Autonomously create new property entities per rules
  • Cannot make investment, financial, or operational decisions

8. Governance & Constraints

  • Must comply with GGP and SL-specific entity rules
  • Maintain immutable logs
  • Ensure correct IDN references

9. MVP Scope

  • Automate entity creation for standard property LLCs
  • Assign canonical IDs
  • Audit logging and notification to governance
