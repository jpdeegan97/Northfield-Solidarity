Market Research & Future Projection Engine (MRFPE)

1. Engine Identity

Engine Name: Market Research & Future Projection Engine

Acronym: MRFPE

Engine Type: Intelligence / Forecasting

2. Mission Statement

Analyze property markets, loan options, and projected cash flows to provide scenario-based forecasts for investment decisions.

3. Core Responsibilities

  • Collect and normalize market and loan data
  • Run scenarios based on financing options and property metrics
  • Forecast ROI, occupancy, and cash flows under different conditions
  • Feed insights to SL management and PTE

4. Non-Responsibilities

  • Create entities (PECA)
  • Execute trades or financial allocations
  • Publish content or manage engagement

5. Inputs

6. Outputs

7. Authority & Decision Rights

  • Autonomously generate forecasts and scenarios
  • Cannot deploy capital or execute purchases
  • Cannot modify entity structure or legal assets

8. Governance & Constraints

  • Forecasting must comply with SL and GGP policies
  • Historical assumptions and inputs must be auditable
  • Scenarios must be clearly documented

9. MVP Scope

  • Limited to 1–2 loan types and key property categories
  • Short- to mid-term projections (1–3 years)
  • Basic scenario reporting dashboards

SL Engine Status: Conceptual for all three engines; MVP scope defined for initial implementation planning.
