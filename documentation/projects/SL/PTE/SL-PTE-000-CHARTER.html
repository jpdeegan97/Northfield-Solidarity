Portfolio Tracker Engine (PTE)

1. Engine Identity

Engine Name: Portfolio Tracker Engine

Acronym: PTE

Engine Type: Asset / Portfolio Management

2. Mission Statement

Maintain awareness of all real estate assets under each entity, including valuation, cash flow, occupancy, and ownership metrics.

3. Core Responsibilities

  • Track all property assets across SL entities
  • Consolidate financial and operational data per asset
  • Generate dashboards, alerts, and summary reports for portfolio management
  • Maintain historical performance of assets

4. Non-Responsibilities

  • Create legal entities (PECA)
  • Govern compliance beyond asset tracking
  • Make investment or financing decisions

5. Inputs

6. Outputs

7. Authority & Decision Rights

  • Autonomously track and report asset status
  • Cannot allocate capital or make investment decisions

8. Governance & Constraints

  • Must comply with GGP-defined reporting standards
  • Maintain historical and immutable records
  • Ensure consistency of asset data across entities

9. MVP Scope

  • Core property tracking per entity
  • Basic valuation and occupancy metrics
  • Dashboard and alert functionality
