1. Purpose

This SOP governs all transactions, services, and financial flows between affiliated entities under the Northfield Solidarity group.

It exists to:

  • Enforce arm’s-length behavior between related entities
  • Prevent commingling of funds or undocumented value transfer
  • Support audit, tax, and compliance defensibility
  • Enable scalable internal services without regulatory exposure

Outcome ensured:

  • Every intercompany transaction is documented, priced, approved, and auditable

2. Scope

Entities Covered

  • Parent / HoldCo (Northfield Solidarity)
  • All operating subsidiaries
  • Any future domestic or foreign affiliated entities

Roles Covered

  • Parent Manager
  • Entity Managers
  • Finance & Accounting
  • Compliance Officer

Activities Included

  • Management fees
  • Shared services billing
  • Cost allocations and reimbursements
  • Intercompany loans (non-capital)
  • IP licensing charges

Explicit Exclusions

  • Equity contributions (see Capital Flow SOP)
  • Third-party vendor payments
  • Owner distributions

3. Definitions

4. Governing Principles

  • All transactions must be arm’s-length
  • No undocumented transfers permitted
  • Written agreements required before execution
  • Finance and Compliance visibility is mandatory
  • Parent retains override authority

5. Roles & Responsibilities

6. Process Overview

Inputs: Service request, cost data, pricing schedules

Key Steps: Agreement → Pricing → Approval → Invoicing → Payment → Reconciliation

Outputs: Recorded intercompany transaction with audit trail

7. Detailed Procedures

7.1 Service or Cost Identification

  • Action: Identify intercompany service or shared cost
  • Owner: Subsidiary or Parent
  • Output: Transaction request

7.2 Pricing Determination

  • Action: Apply approved pricing model or cost allocation basis
  • Owner: Parent Finance
  • Output: Pricing calculation

7.3 Agreement Validation

  • Action: Confirm intercompany agreement exists
  • Owner: Compliance
  • Output: Approved agreement reference

7.4 Invoicing

  • Action: Issue intercompany invoice
  • Owner: Finance
  • Output: Invoice record

7.5 Settlement

  • Action: Execute payment within net terms
  • Owner: Subsidiary Finance
  • Output: Payment confirmation

7.6 Reconciliation

  • Action: Match invoice, payment, and ledger
  • Owner: Finance
  • Output: Closed transaction

8. Controls & Compliance

  • Dual approval for pricing changes
  • Monthly reconciliation required
  • No retroactive adjustments without Parent approval
  • Compliance review of material transactions

9. Records & Documentation

10. KPIs & Monitoring

  • Timeliness of invoicing
  • Reconciliation variance rate
  • Percentage of documented transactions
  • Audit exceptions

11. Exceptions & Escalation

  • Exceptions reported to Compliance
  • Parent approval required for overrides
  • Temporary measures documented and time-bound

12. Tools & Systems

  • Accounting platform
  • Document management system
  • Compliance tracker

13. Security & Access Control

  • Finance edit rights restricted
  • Read-only access for subsidiaries
  • Approval rights limited to Parent

14. Audit & Review

  • Annual review by Finance & Compliance
  • Triggered review upon tax, entity, or regulatory changes
