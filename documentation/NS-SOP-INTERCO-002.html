Inter-Entity Services SOP (v1.0)

Purpose

To define standardized rules for how subsidiary entities provide services to one another, invoice for those services, and exchange value while maintaining legal separation, financial clarity, and operational efficiency.


Governing Priciples

All inter-entity services must adhere to the following principles


  1. Arm's Length Standard
  2. Services are priced as if provided between unrelated parties
  3. No Commingling
  4. Each entity maintains separate accoutns, books, and contracts
  5. Operational Isolation
  6. Liability remains contained with the providing entity
  7. Transparency
  8. All services are documented, invoiced, and auditable
  9. Minimal Parent Involvement
  10. Northfield Solidarity LLC governs but does not provide services.

Prohibited Service Relationships

The following are no permitted

  • Subsidiaries providing services directly to Northfield Solidarity LLC
  • Informal "help" wihtout documentation
  • Paying expenses on behalf of another entity without reimbursement
  • Revenue collection by non-authorized entiteis


Invoicing & Payment SOP


Invoicing Rules

  • Monthly Invoices Only
  • Itemized services
  • Referecne applicable SOP
  • Due within 30 days

Payment Rules

  • Paid via bank transfer
  • No personal funds
  • No shared accounts

Pricing Governance


  • Initial pricingt set by Parent approval
  • Adjustments reviewed quarterly
  • Pricing must remain commercially reasonable
  • Documentation retained

Dispute Resolution

  • Disputes escalated to Parent
  • Temporary payment hold permitted
  • Resolution documented
  • SOP amended if systemic issue found

Compliance & Audit Rights

  • Parent servers audit rights
  • Subsidiaries must retain records for 7 years
  • Non-compliance may results in capital restriction

SOP Evolution & Scaling

As group grows:

  • SOP converts into firmal intercompany agreements
  • Automation introduced (billing, reporting)
  • Management entity may be inserted if justified

Amendment Protocol

This SOP may be amended by:

  • Parent resolution
  • Addition/removal of entities
  • Structural changes
