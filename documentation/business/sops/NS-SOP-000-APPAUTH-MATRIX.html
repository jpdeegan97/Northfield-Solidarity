1. Purpose

This Approval & Authority Matrix defines who is authorized to approve, execute, review, and override actionsacross the Northfield Solidarity entity group.

It exists to:

  • Enforce separation of duties
  • Prevent unauthorized commitments or risk exposure
  • Align approvals with materiality thresholds
  • Translate SOPs into enforceable authority logic

This document is binding and supersedes informal or implied authority.

2. Scope

Covered Areas

  • Financial transactions
  • Intercompany activity
  • Contracts & commitments
  • Tax elections & filings
  • IP & licensing decisions
  • Risk, contingency, and emergency actions

Entities Covered

  • Parent / HoldCo
  • All subsidiaries (current and future)
