1. Purpose

This SOP establishes a minimal, defensible framework for tax positioning and transfer pricing across the Northfield Solidarity group.

It exists to:

  • Prevent accidental tax exposure or misclassification
  • Ensure intercompany pricing is facially defensible
  • Create audit-aligned documentation without operational drag
  • Serve as a foundation for a future robust tax framework

Outcome ensured:

  • Tax posture is intentional, documented, and consistent across entities

2. Scope

Entities Covered

  • Parent / HoldCo
  • All domestic operating subsidiaries
  • Any foreign entities once activated

Activities Included

  • Intercompany pricing alignment
  • IP licensing tax treatment
  • Management fee positioning
  • Expense allocation tax handling

Explicit Exclusions

  • Detailed jurisdiction-by-jurisdiction tax optimization
  • Advanced treaty planning
  • Formal transfer pricing studies (handled in Robust SOP)

4. Governing Principles

  • Substance over form
  • Arm’s-length pricing is mandatory
  • IP income aligns with IP ownership
  • Costs follow value creation
  • Documentation exists before enforcement, not after

5. Roles & Responsibilities

6. Process Overview

Inputs: Intercompany agreements, pricing models, financial data 

Key Steps: Classification → Pricing → Recording → Review 

Outputs: Consistent tax treatment and audit-ready records

7. Lean Procedures

7.1 Intercompany Pricing Alignment

  • Use cost-plus or flat-fee models approved by Parent
  • Apply consistently across periods
  • Avoid retroactive adjustments without explanation

7.2 IP Royalty Positioning

  • Royalties charged only where IP licenses exist
  • Rates set conservatively within market norms
  • Revenue recognized by IP-owning entity

7.3 Expense Allocation Treatment

  • Shared expenses allocated based on reasonable drivers
  • Allocations documented annually

7.4 Jurisdictional Awareness

  • Track where revenue, customers, and operations occur
  • Escalate potential nexus issues to Parent

8. Controls & Compliance

  • Parent approval of pricing models
  • Annual consistency check
  • Alignment with Intercompany, IP, Revenue, and Finance SOPs
