1. Purpose

This SOP governs the movement, control, reconciliation, and oversight of cash and financial transactions across the Northfield Solidarity ecosystem, excluding capital contributions and revenue recognition which are governed by their respective SOPs.

This SOP exists to:

  • Ensure liquidity, solvency, and financial discipline
  • Prevent commingling of funds across entities and purposes
  • Enforce approval, reconciliation, and auditability of cash movements
  • Serve as the operational financial backbone connecting Revenue, Fulfillment, Brand, Capital, and Compliance SOPs

Risks mitigated:

  • Cash leakage or unauthorized disbursements
  • Insolvency due to poor cash forecasting
  • Regulatory or tax exposure from improper transfers
  • Weak internal controls or audit failures

Outcome ensured:

  • Predictable, traceable, and compliant cash operations
  • Clear separation between operating cash, capital, and intercompany flows
  • Real-time visibility into cash position and obligations

2. Scope

Entities Covered:

  • Northfield Solidarity (Parent / HoldCo)
  • All operating subsidiaries
  • All holding entities managing bank accounts or payment instruments

Roles Covered:

  • Finance Lead / Controller
  • Treasury Manager (if applicable)
  • Accounts Payable (AP)
  • Compliance Officer
  • Authorized Signatories

Activities Included:

  • Cash receipts and deposits
  • Operating disbursements and payments
  • Bank account management
  • Cash forecasting and liquidity planning
  • Intercompany cash settlements (non-capital)
  • Reconciliation and reporting
  • Exception handling and escalation

Explicit Exclusions:

  • Capital contributions or equity injections (Capital Flow SOP v1.2)
  • Revenue recognition and AR (Revenue Flow SOP v1.2)
  • Intercompany loans (Intercompany Transactions SOP v1.0)

3. Definitions

4. Governing Principles

  • No commingling of funds between entities or purposes
  • Every cash movement must map to an approved business purpose
  • Dual approval required above defined thresholds
  • Reconciliation is mandatory and non-optional
  • Exceptions route through Compliance SOP v1.2
  • Parent / HoldCo retains override authority for material cash decisions

5. Roles & Responsibilities

6. Process Overview

Inputs:

  • Approved invoices and payment requests
  • Cash receipts from operations
  • Bank statements
  • Forecasting data

Key Steps:

  1. Cash receipt and deposit
  2. Payment request and approval
  3. Cash disbursement
  4. Bank and ledger reconciliation
  5. Cash forecasting
  6. Compliance review and escalation
  7. Documentation storage

Outputs:

  • Updated cash balances
  • Reconciled ledgers
  • Cash flow reports
  • Compliance Event Logs (if applicable)

7. Detailed Procedures

7.1 Cash Receipts

  • Action: Deposit and record operating cash receipts
  • Owner: Finance Lead / AP
  • Output: Recorded cash receipt

7.2 Payment Request & Approval

  • Action: Validate invoice, confirm business purpose, obtain approvals
  • Owner: AP with Finance Lead review
  • Output: Approved payment authorization

7.3 Cash Disbursement

  • Action: Execute payment via approved banking channels
  • Owner: AP
  • Output: Completed payment record

7.4 Intercompany Cash Settlement

  • Action: Settle operating balances between entities
  • Owner: Finance Lead
  • Output: Intercompany settlement entries
  • Compliance: Must not constitute capital or loan without escalation

7.5 Bank & Ledger Reconciliation

  • Action: Reconcile bank statements to ledger monthly
  • Owner: Finance Lead
  • Output: Reconciliation reports

7.6 Cash Forecasting

  • Action: Project cash inflows and outflows
  • Owner: Treasury Manager
  • Output: Rolling cash forecast

7.7 Compliance Escalation

  • Action: Escalate unauthorized payments, shortages, or control failures
  • Owner: Compliance Officer
  • Output: Compliance Event Log and Parent notification

7.8 Documentation Storage

  • Action: Store payment records, approvals, reconciliations
  • Owner: Finance Lead
  • Output: Audit-ready records

8. Controls & Compliance

  • Dual approval thresholds enforced
  • Segregation of duties between AP and Finance Lead
  • Monthly reconciliations mandatory
  • Compliance SOP v1.2 governs all exceptions
  • Annual internal and external audits

9. Records & Documentation

10. KPIs & Monitoring

  • Cash balance accuracy
  • Payment timeliness
  • Forecast variance
  • Unauthorized transaction rate

11. Exceptions & Escalation

  • Unauthorized disbursement: Immediate Compliance escalation
  • Liquidity shortfall: Finance Lead → Parent
  • Intercompany misclassification: Compliance → Parent
  • Emergency payments logged post-event

14. Audit & Review
